## Motor-LOCK guardrails — four layers

1. **Vite outDir is a NEW dedicated subdir** (`www/shell/`) — the build cannot collide with `www/books/**` or `www/index.html`.
2. **`emptyOutDir: false`** — Vite never deletes anything in `www/` (zero risk to books).
3. **Permission deny rules** in `.claude/settings.json` block `Edit`/`Write` on `www/books/**` and each root book dir (unchanged from earlier SUB-PRs).
4. **ESLint + Prettier are scoped to `src-shell/`** via `ignores: ['www/**', ...]` and `.prettierignore` — they never touch books.

Result: `git status www/books` showed **0 modified** before, during, and after every Capacitor and Vite operation in this SUB-PR.
